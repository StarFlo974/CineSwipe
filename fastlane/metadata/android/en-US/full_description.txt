CineSwipe — Discover, Track & Watch

Swipe through movies like you browse social media. Powered by TMDB, CineSwipe serves you trending and curated films based on your genre preferences.

HOW IT WORKS
• Swipe right — add to your watchlist
• Swipe left — mark as seen and rate (1–5 stars)
• Swipe up — skip this movie

FEATURES
• Swipe deck — endless stream of movies from TMDB trending & discover
• Watchlist — save films you want to watch later
• Seen — track every film you've watched with a personal rating
• Statistics — view your average rating and favorite genres at a glance
• Where to watch — see streaming, rental and purchase availability for your country
• Genre filter — narrow the deck to only the genres you love
• Dark & light mode — easy on the eyes, day or night
• No account required — everything stays on your device

PRIVACY
No sign-up, no tracking, no ads. Your watchlist and ratings are stored locally on your device only.

Data provided by TMDB (The Movie Database) — tmdb.org
